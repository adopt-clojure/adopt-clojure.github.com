@{h3-underline Functional}

Clojure provides you with all the necessary tools to allow you to adopt functional programming. Immutability, higher-order functions and avoiding side-effects are central to Clojure's design and philosophy.

@{h3-underline Dynamic}

Clojure is dynamically typed to help you build more flexible systems that better reflect the real world. No more getting stuck trying to build the perfect data-model.

@{h3-underline Concurrent}

Clojure's immutable data structures allow you to share them between thread without worry. However, in the situations where mutation is necessary, Clojure provides you with thread-safe wrappers to ensure that your data is synchronized across threads.

@{h3-underline Runtime Polymorphism}

Systems that utilize runtime polymorphism are easier to change and extend. Clojure offers simple, powerful and flexible mechanisms for runtime polymorphism. @{h3-underline Interactive}

Experience REPL-driven development with Clojure. It allows you to make incremental changes to your system without ever restarting it to maximize your productivity. You can grow your program, with data loaded, adding features, fixing bugs, and testing, in an unbroken stream.

@{h3-underline Hosted}

Clojure is a hosted language - it compiles to your choice of the JVM, JavaScript or dotnet CLR. This gives you access to hundreds of thousands of high-quality libraries that let you always chose the best tool for the job at hand.
